HOW WE WORK?

 
 
 

Ideate

Similar to how important a strong foundation is for a building, the thought process and ideas in ideation phase lay down the base of a strong scalable solution. We discuss, we debate, we collaborate and thus we IDEATE!

 

   
 
 
 

Draft

Once a strong foundation is laid, it is equally important to create a blueprint of the building. Our engineering team challenges the craftsmen within them, to craft the most efficient approach to solve a problem. We design solutions keeping business objectives and technology advancements intact, thus we DRAFT!

 

 
 
 

Execute

After a good design, it is a good implementation with the correct materials and regular monitoring that makes the building a concrete shelter for your loved ones. We at InnovatorsBay use Agile methodology to develop our solutions and products. Design Thinking, Scalability, Sustainability and Quality is our code of conduct to EXECUTE!
